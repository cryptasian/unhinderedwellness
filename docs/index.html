Meet Valerie

My Raindrop Harmonics Journey:

In June 2015 I started reading a book called Life Reimagined. I didn’t finish reading the book, because I came to a place where it wanted me to make decisions on what my reimagined life would be, and I simply could not answer those questions. I had nothing – absolutely nothing to go on. So I prayed asking God for direction, and I decided to wait and see how He would direct me.

Jump ahead to June 2016, when I badly sprained my ankle. Little did I realize at the time, that God was catapulting me into the beginnings of my reimagined life! It was during my recovery that I was introduced to the Sacred Solfeggio frequency through Michael Tyrrell’s WholeTones music project. Then I learned that there are people who use tuning forks in this frequency to do bodywork. (I didn’t even know what “bodywork” was!) Finally, this led me to take classes in Raindrop Harmonics, for the purpose of learning how to use the Sacred Solfeggio frequency to support the physical, emotional, and spiritual body. By the end of July 2016 I attended my first Raindrop Class, and was joyfully overwhelmed by the certainty that “this” was what I was called to. When that calling came to me, I had only sat through 2 hours of chemistry and still hadn’t experienced the actual technique! I didn’t even know what “this” was, but I knew it was connected to my destiny.

Now, 3 years later I am a Raindrop Harmonics Advanced Practitioner, a Licensed Spiritual Healer, a Certified Aromatherapy Coach, and my business, Unhindered Wellness, opened on September 9, 2019!

I am now living a life I could have NEVER imagined! Owning a business based on using therapeutic grade essential oils, tuning forks, colored lights, & gemstones for the glory of God was previously nowhere on my radar! God is so amazing when we partner with Him…trusting Him every step of the way. I’m so thankful to have discovered my purpose…and this is only the beginning!

About

Raindrop Technique is a sequence of anointing with oils and laying on of hands that brings structural and electrical alignment to the body through the power of essential oils. It was developed in the 1980s by Dr. Gary Young.

Raindrop Harmonics (RH) is a combination of multiple vibrational healing modalities such as gemstones, color light, and/or sacred geometry added to the tuning forks used in Vibrational Raindrop Technique. Developed by Christi Bonds-Garrett.

Chromatic Raindrop Technique (CRT), uses color light in place of tuning forks. Combining color light therapy with the essential oils in Raindrop Harmonics adds a subtle but powerful enhancement to the session. Developed by Christi Bonds-Garrett.

There are 9 Essential oils/blends used in a Classic Vibrational Raindrop Technique; Valor, Oregano, Thyme, Basil, Wintergreen, Marjoram, Cypress, Peppermint, and Aroma Siez.
